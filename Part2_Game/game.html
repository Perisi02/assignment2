Instructions

Our waterways are under threat from pollution. Your mission is to help restore the environment by collecting rubbish floating in the water. Each piece you collect helps to make Te Wai-o-Tāne cleaner and healthier for all.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D - Move your character around the waterway
  • Spacebar - Collect rubbish/pollution & resume game when paused
  • ESC - Pause game

Part 2 - Game

About the game

This is an interactive environmental game that challenges the player to protect New Zealand's waterways through action and awareness. Wai (water) is a taonga (possession/treasure) of great importance to many cultures of Aotearoa, including Māori. Te Wai-o-Tane want to use this game to encourage cultural values of respect for Awa (rivers) and Wai-Tai (sea).
